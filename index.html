Confidential & Secure

Referral Portal

For licensed caseworkers and placement professionals only. Submit a youth's confidential clinical files for admission consideration at Cool Living Quarters.

This is a confidential intake portal. All information submitted here is protected and used solely for placement consideration. Do not share access to this portal with unauthorized individuals. All submissions are reviewed by our clinical intake team within 24 hours.

✅ This portal is for:

  • Texas DFPS caseworkers
  • Child Protective Services (CPS) staff
  • Juvenile probation officers
  • Court-appointed placement specialists
  • Licensed child-placing agencies (CPAs)
  • Authorized STAR Health / MCO representatives

❌ This portal is NOT for:

  • General public inquiries
  • Parents or family members acting independently
  • Youth seeking self-placement
  • Unauthorized third parties
